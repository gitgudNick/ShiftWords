ShiftWords — Account Removal Request

Submitting this form will permanently delete your ShiftWords account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

What will be deleted:

  • Your sign-in account (Google / Apple)
  • Your cloud save (progress, stats, coins)
  • Your leaderboard scores and daily challenge history

What will not be deleted:

  • Local game data stored on your device — you can clear this via Android Settings → Apps → ShiftWords → Clear Data
