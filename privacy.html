Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

ShiftWords ("the App") is developed and published independently. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

Account & Game Data
When you use the App we store:

  • A display name you choose
  • A unique player ID generated on your device
  • Game progress: levels completed, scores, stars, streaks
  • In-game currency (coins) balance
  • Unlocked cosmetics (tile sets)

Usage & Analytics
Google Firebase Analytics automatically collects:

  • App events (level started/completed, modes played)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and session duration

This data is aggregated and used only to understand how the game is played so we can improve it. We do not sell analytics data.

Advertising
The App shows optional rewarded advertisements via Google AdMob. When you choose to watch an ad, AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (resettable in your device settings)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • App usage context for ad relevance

You are never required to watch ads. Ads are only loaded when you tap the ad button. You can reset your Advertising ID at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To save and restore your game progress
  • To display leaderboard scores (display name + score only)
  • To operate the Friends feature (display name + scores)
  • To serve rewarded ads and award in-game coins
  • To improve gameplay through anonymised analytics

We do not use your data for marketing emails, profiling, or sale to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Firebase Analytics — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Supabase (cloud data storage for leaderboards, friends, and optional account sync) — supabase.com/privacy

4. Data Storage

Local storage: Game saves are written to your device and are not transmitted unless you use the cloud sync feature.

Cloud storage: If you use leaderboards, the Friends feature, or cloud save backup, your display name, player ID, and scores are stored on Supabase servers located in the European Union.

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. If you have not played in 12 months your data may be deleted from our servers. Local device data is always under your control.

5. Children's Privacy

ShiftWords is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Advertising Consent (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the European Economic Area, UK, or other region with consent requirements, you will be asked for consent before personalised ads are shown. You may change your consent at any time via the ad settings on your device or by contacting us.

California residents may opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted here with a revised date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
